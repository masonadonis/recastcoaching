To my athletes,

I want to be straight with you, because that's the only way I know how to operate. I'm officially parting ways with Vertis Coaching. It's a clean break, made on good terms, and I'm grateful for the time I spent there. But this letter isn't about closing a door. It's about what's on the other side of it.

First, the thing that matters most. You and I are not going anywhere. Your training doesn't change. Your programming doesn't change. The standards we've built together, the early mornings, the hard conversations about pacing and load, the stuff that actually moved the needle. None of that disappears because my business address did. You signed on to work with me, and I'm still here.

"The relationship doesn't live in a company name. It lives in the work."

I want to say thank you. And I mean it. You trusted me with something real. Your time, your goals, the version of yourself you're still building. Watching you get faster, get tougher, figure out who you are when the miles get long. I carry that with me. You've made me a better coach, and I don't take that lightly.

I came to coaching the long way. I was a 250 lb offensive lineman who walked onto a college football team, and by the time I graduated, I was 60 lbs lighter and lining up as a wide receiver. Then I picked up distance running from scratch and eventually qualified for Boston with a 2:52 finish. I didn't have a blueprint. I had stubbornness and a willingness to learn.

That's the lens I bring to every training plan I write. There's no entitlement to performance. You earn it, rep by rep, mile by mile, decision by decision. And when I coach you, I'm not managing your expectations. I'm building your capacity.

What's next

Operating independently means I can build the coaching experience around you, not around a company's structure or product roadmap. Recast Coaching is exactly that. A practice designed from the ground up to go deeper, move faster, and actually fit the way serious athletes train. There's more coming. Tools, resources, and things I've been building quietly that I think you're going to find genuinely useful. I'll share more soon.

Right now, we keep working. You keep showing up. I keep showing up. The goal doesn't move just because the scenery changed.

I'm fired up about what's ahead, more than I've been in a long time. The best is in front of us. Let's go get it.

Mason Jordan
